About

I am a graduate student at the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), currently pursuing a Master's degree in Aerospace Engineering. My research interests lie at the intersection of structural mechanics, design, and advanced manufacturing.


Prior to graduate school, I completed my Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering at KAIST, where I also participated in UROP (Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program) projects that shaped my passion for engineering research.


Beyond academia, I am passionate about creative work — whether it is crafting objects by hand, sketching and illustrating, or capturing moments through photography. I believe that creativity and engineering complement each other deeply.
